The Crossing

This bridging service provides access to inter-blockchain liquidity. The platform currently supports bi-directional swaps between Ethereum main-net and Binance Smart Chain.

Users can easily bridge supported tokens by connecting their metamask wallets, selecting the blockchain they wish to swap from and to and filling the amount they want to swap. Once the process is complete funds will be deposited in the users wallet. Gas fees and bridge fee apply, please see whitepaper for full details.
